# Google Play Store — Upload Instructions
# For: Computer Use Agent

## Goal
Upload all store listing assets for the **Aagam Holidays** app on Google Play Console.

---

## Step 1 — Open Google Play Console

1. Navigate to: https://play.google.com/console
2. Sign in if prompted.
3. In the app list, click **Aagam Holidays**.

---

## Step 2 — Open Store Listing

1. In the left sidebar, click **Grow** → **Store presence** → **Main store listing**.
2. Make sure the language selected at the top is **English (United Kingdom) – en-GB**.

---

## Step 3 — Upload App Icon

**Requirement:** PNG, 512 × 512 px, up to 1 MB.

**File:** `mobile/marketing/play-store/icon/icon-512.png`

1. Scroll to the **Graphics** section.
2. Find **App icon** and click **Upload icon** (or the existing icon to replace it).
3. Upload the file above.
4. Wait for the preview to appear and confirm it looks correct (orange gradient icon).

---

## Step 4 — Upload Feature Graphic

**Requirement:** PNG or JPEG, 1024 × 500 px, up to 15 MB.

**File:** `mobile/marketing/play-store/feature-graphic/feature-graphic.png`

1. Find **Feature graphic** and click **Upload feature graphic**.
2. Upload the file above.
3. Confirm the preview shows the orange branded banner with "Plan better trips. Stay connected on the go."

---

## Step 5 — Upload Phone Screenshots

**Requirement:** PNG, 1080 × 1920 px each, 9:16 ratio. Upload all 4.

**Files (in order):**
| # | File |
|---|------|
| 1 | `mobile/marketing/play-store/phone/01-home.png` |
| 2 | `mobile/marketing/play-store/phone/02-explore.png` |
| 3 | `mobile/marketing/play-store/phone/03-destinations.png` |
| 4 | `mobile/marketing/play-store/phone/04-profile.png` |

**Steps:**
1. Scroll to the **Phone** section under **Screenshots**.
2. Click **Upload phone screenshots**.
3. Select all 4 files at once (or one by one).
4. After upload, drag to reorder them in the order listed above (01 first, 04 last).
5. Confirm all 4 previews are visible with real app content.

---

## Step 8 — Save

1. Scroll to the bottom of the page.
2. Click **Save** (blue button, bottom right).
3. Wait for the confirmation banner: *"Store listing saved"*.

---

## Verification Checklist

After saving, confirm the following are all showing correctly:

- [ ] App icon — orange gradient icon visible
- [ ] Feature graphic — orange banner with app tagline
- [ ] Phone screenshots — 4 images, starting with home screen
- [ ] 7-inch tablet screenshots — 4 images
- [ ] 10-inch tablet screenshots — 4 images
- [ ] No red error banners on the page
- [ ] Status shows no pending errors in the left sidebar

---
